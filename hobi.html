HOBI SAYA

I have lots of hobbies, but cooking is the one that I like the most. When I have free time, I like to cook. I’ve been into cooking since I was a little. Back then, I often saw my father cooking. Most of the time I cook alone, but sometimes my father helps me. He’s actually the one who teaches me how to cook. I really love cooking because it makes me happy. I feel proud whenever see someone eating the food I make, especially when they praise the food. I hope someday I can be an excellent cook like my father.
